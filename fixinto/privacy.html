FixinTo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

The short version: Scotty Software never sees your tasks, never stores your data on our servers, and never sells your information. Your tasks go directly from your device to your own Google account. That's it.

What FixinTo Does

FixinTo is a voice-first task manager that lets you speak or type tasks and have them appear on your Google Calendar or Apple Calendar. It is developed by Scotty Software and is provided free of charge.

What Data We Collect

Scotty Software collects no data whatsoever. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics. We cannot see your tasks, your Google account information, or any other personal information.

Google Account Access

FixinTo requests access to your Google account solely to create and manage tasks in Google Tasks on your behalf. Specifically:

  • We request the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/tasks scope to create, complete, and delete tasks
  • Your Google credentials are managed entirely by Google's own sign-in system
  • We never see, store, or transmit your Google password or authentication tokens
  • Tasks are written directly from your device to your Google account — Scotty Software is never in the middle

Google's privacy policy governs how Google handles your data. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

Apple Calendar Access (iOS)

On iPhone and iPad, FixinTo optionally requests access to your device calendar to create all-day events alongside your Google Tasks. This allows tasks to appear in Apple Calendar. Calendar data is written locally to your device and synced by Apple's own iCloud infrastructure. Scotty Software never receives or stores any calendar data.

Voice Data

When you use the voice input feature, speech recognition is performed on your device using your device's built-in speech recognition. Audio is not recorded, stored, or transmitted by Scotty Software.

Local Storage

FixinTo stores your preferences (such as your default task list and display settings) locally on your device using standard platform storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third Party Services

FixinTo uses Google Sign-In and the Google Tasks API. These services are governed by Google's own terms of service and privacy policy. FixinTo does not use any advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

FixinTo does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app requires a Google account which itself requires users to be at least 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of FixinTo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@scottysoftware.com.
