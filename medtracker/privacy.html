MedTracker Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Introduction

MedTracker is developed by Scott's IT Solutions. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how MedTracker handles your medical information.

Data Collection

MedTracker collects ZERO data from you.

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal or medical information. Everything stays on your device.

What Information MedTracker Stores

MedTracker stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Person names and dates of birth
  • Medication information
  • Doctor contact information
  • Medical conditions
  • Allergy information
  • Appointment details
  • PDF documents you choose to export

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device
  • No cloud sync
  • No remote servers
  • No data transmission to Scott's IT Solutions or any third parties
  • Biometric security (fingerprint, face ID, or device PIN/password) is required to access the app and protects your data using your device's secure hardware

Permissions

MedTracker may request the following permissions:

Biometric / Device Credentials - REQUIRED

Required to access MedTracker. You must authenticate using fingerprint, face ID, or your device PIN/password every time you open the app. This ensures your medical information remains private even if your phone is unlocked. Biometric data never leaves your device's secure hardware.

Camera - Optional

Used for OCR scanning of prescription bottles and business cards. Images are processed locally on your device and are not saved or transmitted. You can use MedTracker without granting camera permission.

Calendar - Optional

Only used if you choose to sync appointments to your device calendar. Your calendar data is not accessed or transmitted except for appointments you explicitly create.

Storage - Optional

Only used for importing/exporting backup files that you explicitly choose to create.

Exports and Backups

When you export data:

  • You control where the file goes (email, cloud storage, local storage, etc.)
  • You choose between standard or encrypted exports
  • Encrypted exports use AES-256 encryption with your password
  • Standard exports are unencrypted JSON or PDF files
  • We cannot access your exported files (encrypted or not)

Important: Exported files remain wherever you store them (email, Google Drive, Downloads folder, etc.) even after you delete MedTracker. You are responsible for managing and deleting your exported backup files.

We recommend using encrypted exports when:

  • Storing backups in cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
  • Sending backups via email
  • Sharing backups across devices
  • Any situation where you want additional security

Standard (unencrypted) exports are useful when:

  • Using our desktop HTML import tool
  • You need to manually edit the JSON file
  • Importing into other compatible systems

Third-Party Services

MedTracker uses:

  • Google ML Kit - For OCR text recognition from prescription bottles and business cards. All processing happens entirely on your device.
  • Google Play Services - For app distribution and in-app purchases only.

Neither service receives your medical data.

Children's Privacy

MedTracker can be used to track children's medical information by parents/guardians. We do not collect any information from children.

Data Deletion

To delete your data stored within MedTracker:

  1. Uninstall MedTracker from your device
  2. All data stored in the app is permanently deleted

Note: If you have exported backup files (JSON or PDF), those files remain in the locations where you saved them (email, cloud storage, Downloads folder, etc.). You must manually delete those exported files if you no longer want them.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You own all your medical information
  • You can export it anytime
  • You can delete it anytime (both from the app and any exported files)
  • We never see or access your data

Summary: MedTracker is privacy-first. Your medical data stays on YOUR device, under YOUR control, forever.
